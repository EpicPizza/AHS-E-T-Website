### Impacts and Awards

- Innovation in Control Award at 2024 Hawaii Regional
- 2x Alliance Captain at 2024 Hawaii and San Francisco Regionals
- Semifinalists at 2024 Idaho Regional
- Collaborated with a variety of local organizations, including all-stars, salvation army, and local FLL teams, to host summer camps for students grade 1-9

### Activities 

- Training/Competition preparation meetings twice a week
- Various outreach/volunteering/teaching events
- Participation in CalGames (offseason) and yearly FRC competition
