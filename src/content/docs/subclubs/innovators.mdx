### Quick Facts

import { Card, CardGrid, LinkCard } from '@astrojs/starlight/components';

<Card title="Mission" icon="rocket"> 
    AHS Innovators' Club give students of each level activities and lessons in STEM. Members will make physical projects combined with design and engineering. Students will be taught use of Tinkercad Arduinos, C++, assistive technology, and robotics.
</Card>
<Card title="Plans" icon="open-book"> 
    AHS innovators' club will teach introduction to robotics, arduinos, C++, resistors, engineering, led circuit, physical testing and online in Tinkercad.
</Card>
