- Collaboration with TheCodingSchool's QubitXQubit Program for building quantum transistors
- Collaboration with Quantum Forum team to shape the narrative of quantum computing
- Learning basics through IBM Quantum Computing Real Time simulator
- Watching lectures from Berkeley Quantum Computing Club
