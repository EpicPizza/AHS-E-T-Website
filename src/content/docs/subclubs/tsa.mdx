### Quick Facts

import { Card, CardGrid, LinkCard } from '@astrojs/starlight/components';

<Card title="Mission" icon="rocket"> 
    The AHS Technology Student Association is associated with the international organization. Our chapter allows students to participate in tech and science competitions at the state and national level. 
</Card>
<Card title="Plans" icon="open-book">
    We plan on continuing participating in States and Nationals and holding weekly progress check meetings. Apart from existing events, we plan on hosting fundraisers/activities with members to raise funds for the national conference to allow more students to participate. We will also be hosting technology bowl events during meetings for increased practice for competitors. 
</Card>
